FAQ

1. Who develops these games?

All the games are made by a solo indie developer under Mayagrid Studio. From coding to design to publishing — everything is built with passion and patience.

2. Are your games free to play?

Yes! All current games are free to download and play. Some may include optional ads or in-game purchases to support development, but core gameplay is always free.

3. I found a bug / issue in the game. What should I do?

Sorry about that! Please reach out through Instagram or email (check the Contact page). Describe the bug and your device — it really helps me fix things faster.

4. Do your games work offline?

Most games work without internet after downloading. Some features like rewards or ads may need connection, but core gameplay is offline-friendly.
